var check_buttons = function () {

//Get all the radio buttons

//We should get NOTHING if we've filled in every option

var unfilled = n_ckd.filter(function (x) { return !ckd.includes(x) });

}

<h3>Below is a list of recordings organized in rows. In each row, you will see two clips: A & B.

Please listen to the two recordings, and then decide whether the two voices in clip A and clip B are the same or different speakers. 

Once you choose, please rate how confident you are in your choice.</h3>

<ul>

<li>There are seven (7) levels of confidence, as shown in the tables below. 

"|" means that your confidence level is between two levels.

more than "Not at all confident" but less than "Somewhat confident".</li>

<li>Please <em><strong><span style="color:red">ignore</span></strong></em> the audio quality

(e.g., noises, distortions) and instead focus on the <em><strong><span style="color:red">voice

<li>You may listen to a sound more than once if needed; just click the play button to replay.</li>

<li>Your responses will be manually screened to ensure that you made a serious effort to rate the recordings. 

If your answers are determined to be random or contain inconsistent answers, the task may be rejected and you may not receive payment.</li>

<li>Please perform these tasks in a quiet place using a pair of good headphones. 

It is recommended that you set the volume a bit higher than normal for these tasks.</li>
