Emotion Perception Test

Below is a list of recordings organized in rows. In each row, you will see two clips: A & B. Please listen to both recordings and decide which clip better matches the target emotion shown in the question (Happy, Angry, Sad, or Neutral). Once you choose, please rate how confident you are in your choice.

  • The target emotion for each row will be one of four categories: Happy, Angry, Sad, or Neutral.
  • Focus on how the person sounds (their tone of voice and expressiveness), rather than the words being said.
  • There are seven (7) levels of confidence, as shown in the tables below. "|" means that your confidence level is between two levels. For example, the "|" between "Not at all confident" and "Somewhat confident" means that your confidence level is more than "Not at all confident" but less than "Somewhat confident".
  • You may listen to a clip more than once if needed; click the play button to replay.
  • Your responses will be manually screened to ensure that you made a serious effort to rate the recordings. If your answers are determined to be random or contain inconsistent answers, the task may be rejected and you may not receive payment.
  • Please perform these tasks in a quiet place using a pair of good headphones. It is recommended that you set the volume a bit higher than normal for these tasks.
  • If you are having trouble viewing or listening to the sound clips, please try completing the test in Google Chrome.
  • Please make sure that you've finished all the questions before submitting.

In each question, choose which clip (A or B) better matches the target emotion. Then rate how confident you are in your answer.
